We have great news for you: Meet Ubidots STEM

Ubidots for Education has grown to a community of over 48,000 IoT enthusiasts like yourself, serving as a place to learn, test and share ideas, resources and projects.

During the past 2 years, we also launched a new and improved platform - Ubidots Industrial - which had only been available to commercial IoT applications so far.

We're now taking the best of both worlds and calling it "Ubidots STEM": a non-commercial product with the purpose-driven and educational nature of Ubidots for Education, and the power of Ubidots Industrial.

We can't wait to see what you build!
